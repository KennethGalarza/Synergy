Features

Friends

Connect with people in your area with synergy. With the focus of synergy being exercise, you can utilize the application for keeping up with eachothers progress, schedule workouts, sharing your achievements and motivating eachother on our exercise journey.

Workouts

Need to figure out a workout routine? Synergy is here for you by providing you with the latest workout routines and our mission system. Workouts provided from you, our members, to share with eachother to challenge your friends. Complete missions and share your achievements.

Rewards

Synergy brings you a reward system for completing missions! Complete missions to earn synergy coins and use them to redeem different items, ranging from exercise equipment, prepaid cards, merchandise, and coupons for fresh produce!
